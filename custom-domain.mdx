## Setting up your SRV record.

To setup your SRV record on your domain, visit your DNS management provider.\
In our example we are using Cloudflare's DNS Manager.

1: Add a new record with SRV as type.

![Screenshot 2025-11-15 184053.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-15184053.png)

2: Fill out the necessary fields for SRV record.

<Accordion title="Information about fields." icon="sparkles">
  1.  **Name**-\> in **Name** field you can add your service name and subdomain, where \_minecraft.tcp is the service paramaters and .play is your subdomain of choice.
  2. **Port** -\> in **Port** field please add your LaunchMC connection port, for example 1002, you can find it on server overview as show in image below.

     <img src="/images/Screenshot2025-11-15184901.png" alt="Screenshot 2025-11-15 184901.png" /> 3.  **Target** -\> in Target field you either add our global IP which route based on GeoDNS as global.launchmc.com or you can manually run latency tests and pick a specific location by going on the connection page. For the image above our link for connection page is **testset.launchmc.com.**
</Accordion>

![Screenshot 2025-11-15 183413.png](/images/Screenshot2025-11-15183413.png)

Thats it! your SRV record will now work as intended!
